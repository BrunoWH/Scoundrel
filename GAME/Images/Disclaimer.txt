You can use you own images by replacing the images here with the EXACT same name.
You can also use custom deck images. To do that, just:
1 - Create a CustomDeck #Must have 'Deck' in the name folder, or it won't recognize!
2 - Add all the cards and the back of the card image. Make sure the name of the cards are the 
same as the ones used in the default decks #they are Always the same names.

#Note: not every card of a deck is used. You can use check the ones NOT used in the _NotUsed folder.
